About Us

Global Changers is deeply committed to empowering youth voices and driving community change at national, regional, and international levels worldwide. We firmly believe that listening to and incorporating youth perspectives into policymaking is essential, as young voices are a valuable asset.

Our core values center around promoting constructive, positive, and peaceful advocacy. Through our initiatives, we tackle real-world challenges with a collaborative approach to address global issues collectively. We not only focus on empowering youth in a structured manner but also recognize the strength found in collaboration and cooperation.

At Global Changers, our efforts are directed towards the sustainable development goals, democracy, human rights, and the rule of law. We are continuously refining our strategies and expanding our focus areas to effectively address current and pressing global issues, while fostering stronger youth connections on a global scale. Join us in being catalysts for positive change at Global Changers.

Climate Change

Our Climate Change project is carried out in Lalitpur Metropolitan City. Our team of young eco-superheros (our facilitators) leads engaging workshops to educate both community members and students from government schools about important climate change issues. Recognizing that many children in government schools come from underprivileged backgrounds with limited digital skills, we have successfully integrated digital training into our sessions.

To learn more about our Climate Change project and to get involved, please send us a message.


Mental Health

Our mental health project focuses on employed adults up to the age of 40. We conduct insightful interviews with leaders to understand how they prioritize employee mental health. These interviews are then compiled into comprehensive research papers aimed at raising awareness about mental health in the workplace. By sharing these findings, we aspire to inspire other companies to follow and prioritize the well-being of their employees.

To learn more about our Mental Health project and to get involved, please send us a message.


Democracy

Ensuring that individuals understand their rights and responsibilities is crucial for maintaining a just society. Our project emphasizes the importance of democracy and the rule of law, aiming to educate youth and the wider community. We gather youth voices on these topics and conduct awareness sessions to ensure that rights, along with a sense of responsibility, are understood, with the vision to uphold democracy and the rule of law.

To learn more about our Democracy project and to get involved, please send us a message.

Publication
