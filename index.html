About Us

Global Changers is driven by the boundless passion and energy of young visionaries who believe that transformative change begins with each individual, particularly the youth. Through our innovative programs and initiatives, we equip young leaders with the tools, resources, and platform they need to unleash their full potential. While our roots may be in Nepal, our impact knows no bounds. We envision igniting change on a global scale, uniting a diverse network of changemakers from every corner of the world.

Get Involved

INITIATIVES

We invite individuals and organizations to join us on our mission to create a more sustainable and equitable world, collaborating together with us. Let's harness the power of collective action and innovation to tackle the challenges of our time.

VOLUNTEER

We are calling upon volunteers worldwide. At Global Changers, volunteering goes beyond just lending a hand. It's about empowering you to become a leader in your own right. We provide comprehensive training to our volunteers, equipping them with the skills and knowledge needed to lead and facilitate various awareness programs.

CONTRIBUTE

Thank you for supporting us. We rely on the generosity of individuals like you to fuel our mission of creating positive change in the world. Your support, no matter the size, can make a significant difference in our ability to implement meaningful projects and initiatives.
